courses

Learn how to create handmade natural soaps from scratch!

I teach the cold-process method of soapmaking, used for thousands of years by combining alkali with fats and scenting with essential oils. In this workshop you will learn how to make cold-process soaps from scratch using ingredients found in your storecupboard. This course is designed for complete beginners so no prior knowledge or experience is required. I'll teach you the basics on how to make soap and provide you with the knowledge to confidently experiment further with it!

The course lasts approximately 3 hours and you will make about 500g of your own soap to take home. Protective equipment will be provided, though the process can get messy so wear appropriate clothing

Strictly over 18's only.
